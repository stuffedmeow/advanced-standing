FRUITS OF MY LABOR
Deviled Eggs
[ Summer 2020 ]

Goal: Create a looped animation

I took an online class on animation in the summer of 2020. From that course I benefitted from being in a educational environment with a variety of other artists. Having to cook at home a lot more than usual as well due to the quarantine, “Deviled Eggs” was the result.

Flailing
[ Fall 2020 ]

Prompt: n/a

“Flailing” depicts me jumping down from the top of my staircase and climbing back up the stairs, continually for 5 minutes. I wanted to this piece to convey the feeling of stress in a domestic space, but with no other choice but emotional stasis in repetitive action.

Created for my Performance Art class.

Synonymous
[ Fall 2020 ]

Prompt: n/a

“Synonymous” presents two bodies deemed biologically opposite, as one in the same through the use of overlapped video and the intimate action of washing.

Created for my Performance Art class.

For Hire
[ Spring 2019 ]

Prompt: Design a Business Card

My first semester as a New Media student, I took Design Principals with Neva Kocic. For one of our assignments we created business cards for ourselves. I envisioned a design for myself as a character designer.
