PHOTOGRAPHY
Adjustment
[ Spring 2020 ]

Prompt: Final Project

My concept for this project was to focus on the effect that the sudden COVID-19 quarantine had on my family members. I made collages of their physical habits during this time, and overlayed images as visual representations of these effects.

Fish
[ Spring 2020 ]
Prompt: Portrait

Using various stockings I owned, I wanted to capture new characters created for my lens. Photographing each individually and as a collective, participants expressed uniquely and interacted differently, taking on personalities along with donning the garments.

Slices
[ Spring 2020 ]

Prompt: Self-Portrait

For this prompt I wanted to portray myself through the lens of physical identifiers. Through a mediation of scars, a hiding of body, and various limbs, the viewer can see me the way I see myself: in slices.
