VIDEO ART
Canvas
[ Fall 2019 ]

Prompt: Final Project

My concept for this project was to create a narrative piece that would help me express my feelings as a non-binary individual when presenting as femme. This piece touches on performing gender and the male gaze.

Home
[ Fall 2019 ]

Prompt: Manifesto Project

For this piece I wanted to convey the homesickness I was feeling in Fall 2019 through the screen. The piece starts by outlining my everyday life, and transitions to memories and thoughts about my baby sister back home.

Warm
[ Fall 2019 ]

Prompt: Performing for the Screen

My concept for this project was to create a mood piece to express the feeling of suffocation that results from the loneliness of every day, even while living in the city. This piece touches on themes of isolation, performance, and conformity.
